Parallels

This inspiration

Christian Löffler, the German musician and producer, found his inspiration for the album “Parallels” in the greats of classical music such as Johann Sebastian Bach, Beethoven and Chopin. He rearranged their works in his own way, creating a unique blend of neoclassical, ambient and electronic music.Inspired by the masters of classical music, Christian Löffler has transformed their heritage into a modern electronic symphony with «Parallels».

For this project, he used 100-year-old classical music recordings from Deutsche Grammophon’s shellac archives. Listening to these recordings, he was overwhelmed by positive emotions and imagined what it would have been like to be in Berlin in the 1930s, attending a concert at the Staatsoper. Thus, the album “Parallels” is a bold exploration of classical music through the prism of electronic music, creating a harmonious dialogue between past and present.
